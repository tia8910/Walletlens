## Tips for high response rates

1. **Be first** — HARO queries fill up fast. Set up email alerts and respond within 2 hours.
2. **Match the angle** — read the journalist's exact question and mirror their framing. Don't send a generic pitch.
3. **Be quotable** — write sentences they can lift directly into the article. Short, punchy, specific.
4. **Include the URL** — always end with walletlens.live on its own line.
5. **Follow up once** — if your response was used, tweet/email the journalist to say thanks. Builds a relationship for future queries.
6. **Target publications** — NerdWallet, Forbes Advisor, Investopedia, Business Insider, CoinDesk, Decrypt, BeInCrypto, The Block all use HARO sources regularly.
