# WalletLens — YouTube Shorts / TikTok Scripts

Three 30-second scripts. Film as a vertical screen-recording (9:16).
Burn in captions — 80 % of views are muted.
Post the same clip to YouTube Shorts + TikTok + Instagram Reels.
Put `walletlens.live` in bio AND first comment on every platform.

---

## Script A — "No signup" hook *(broadest reach)*

| Time | Action | On-screen text |
|------|--------|----------------|
| 0:00 | Black screen or app logo | **"Every net worth tracker wants your email. This one doesn't."** |
| 0:02 | Open walletlens.live in browser — straight to dashboard, no login prompt | "No account. No login. I just opened it." |
| 0:07 | Add a Bitcoin holding → cut to adding Apple stock → cut to adding gold. Fast. | "Crypto, stocks, gold — all in one screen." |
| 0:18 | Pan the dashboard: total net worth figure + allocation chart live | "That's my entire net worth. Live prices." |
| 0:25 | Hold on the WalletLens logo / URL | "Free. Nothing to sign up for. Link in bio." |

**Voiceover tone:** calm, slightly disbelieving — like you just found something you didn't think existed.

## Production checklist

- [ ] Record at 1080 × 1920 (9:16 vertical)
- [ ] Keep total duration ≤ 30 s (YouTube Shorts algorithm cap for max reach)
- [ ] Burn in captions with a large, high-contrast font (CapCut auto-captions works)
- [ ] First 1.5 seconds must show the hook text — no logo intros
- [ ] CTA in last 3 seconds: say the URL out loud + show it on screen
- [ ] Upload same file to: YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels
- [ ] Add `walletlens.live` to bio on all three platforms
- [ ] Pin a comment on each post: "Free, no account: walletlens.live"
- [ ] Post time: Tue–Thu, 7–9 pm local audience time

## Series ideas (one Short per feature)

- Voice import: "I just logged a trade by talking to my phone"
- Screenshot import: "I uploaded a screenshot of my Binance holdings and it read every trade"
- Whale tracker: "This shows you every time a Bitcoin whale moves money — live"
- Offline: "The only portfolio tracker that works with no internet"
- Backup: "I moved my entire portfolio to a new phone in 10 seconds"
